General Instructions (Eduquity Pattern):

  1. Total duration of the examination is 60 minutes.
  2. The countdown timer in the top left corner of the screen will display the remaining time available. When the timer reaches zero, the examination will submit automatically.
  3. The Question Palette displayed on the left side of screen will show the status of each question using one of the following symbols:
    • 1 You have not visited the question yet.
    • 3 You have not answered the question.
    • 5 You have answered the question.
    • 7 You have marked the question for review.
    • 9 Answered and Marked for Review (will be considered for evaluation).
  4. Important: The action buttons like "Save & Next" and "Mark for Review" are located at the Top Right of the question area.

Exam Customization

1. Sectional Timing

Enable a strict timer for each section. Once enabled, you cannot jump between sections, and each section will auto-submit when its time is up.

2. Attempt Order

Customize the sequence in which you want to attempt the sections.

Other Important Instructions

  1. This test consists of 100 questions.
  2. Each correct answer fetches 2 marks.
  3. There is a negative marking of 0.50 marks for each wrong answer.
  4. Navigation: You can navigate between sections as per your choice. If strict navigation is enabled, you cannot jump between sections until the sectional time ends.
